Second: for the first time ever, there is a way out.  
You can be free from all the internet you never asked for.

Haven let's you pick any combination of content categories with a few mouse clicks.
It uses up to 47 independent strategies to keep unwanted content off your network.

What once took corporate network engineers untold hours, our clients can do in a minute.
Suddenly, the internet -- your internet -- the internet you paid for finally listens to you.
You get to decide what stays out and what stays in.

If this excites you even a fraction as much as it excites us, we invite you to witness the dawn of HavenNet.

Haven is to your internet what your security system is to your home.

Inside your home is your HavenNet.

About us

Lulhaven (Digital Technology Services, LLC) was founded in 2007, but we go back much further. Our work is shaped by decades of hands‑on experience with computers, networks, software development, and systems engineering — from early‑era computing to modern secure infrastructure. We’ve been building and fixing technology since long before it was simple.

Our Founder
Lulhaven was created by Dave, an armed forces veteran and a veteran of the industry with decades of diverse experience. His background includes degrees in Electronics Engineering Technology and Computer Science, early‑era networking, Certified NetWare Engineer work, and serving as chair of a university technology department. He founded Digital Technology Services, LLC (DBA Lulhaven) in 2007 and continues to design and build every Lulhaven system with proven, real-world experience.
